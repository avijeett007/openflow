--- (c) macOS leak-through (known upstream gap, NOT fixed here) ---
handy-keys 0.3.0 macOS event tap uses CGEventTapOptions::Default (blocking-capable), but in
src/platform/macos/listener.rs the OtherMouseDown/OtherMouseUp arms send the KeyEvent to the
channel and NEVER call state.should_block(...) — only the keyboard KeyDown/KeyUp/FlagsChanged
arms do. So a mouse side-button bound as a hotkey is DETECTED but NEVER suppressed on macOS:
the click passes through to the frontmost app. This is the complementary macOS side of the
Windows-only suppression fix that PR #14 (issue #12) landed. Documenting only; out of scope.
